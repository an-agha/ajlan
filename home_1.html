WHY AL AJLLAN?

HONEST & DEPENDABLE: Integrity and fairness are the basis of Al Ajllan business ethics, whether dealing with customers, suppliers or fellow team members. Honesty is not the best policy, but the only policy.

EXPERIENCE:The Managers of Al Ajllan and their Employees have many years of experience and a proven track record. Their experience ensures that their projects will be done right and with the upmost professionalism. They also use only proven and reputable subcontractors and material suppliers. Their staff also includes a licensed professional engineers and workers.

OFFICE MANAGEMENT:Besides providing outstanding field work, Al Ajllan also completes the management of their projects with the upmost professionalism. Detailed estimating, proposals, permitting, and material/Subcontractor coordination are essential to a successful project.

COST:Al Ajllan strives to assure their clients real value for their money at a profit sufficient to provide for the well-being of their employees. Their detailed proposals ensure clients that there won’t be any hidden cost and they know exactly what they are getting.

QUALITY:Al Ajllan prides themselves with having a strong dedication to the principle that a quality product is its own best form. Their employees are from the best craftsman in the area. Al Ajllan team gives the best to provide the best quality to the customers obeying the company rules and the quality control system.

SCHEDULE:When Al Ajllan starts a project they commit themselves to get it done in the timeliest manner as possible. They use the latest tools, techniques, and technology to do the job more quickly. By doing their own site work, rough metalwork, fabrication, exterior finish work, and interior finish work they have better control of the project schedule and can expedite the project.

DESIGN SERVICES:Many of the projects that Al Ajllan completes are designed by them using AutoCad, 3D Max, and more as per the project requirement.

By completing drawings in house, they can ensure that the project is designed to your specifications and budget. They can also ensure cost effective designs. They want the client to be happy with their project, now and long into the future. Detailed plans also allow the project to be more precisely estimated and eliminates questions in the field.

SERVICE:Providing clients a complete service package, Al Ajllan guides and Assists clients through every step of the process, from design to material discussion, budget to site arrangement. They consider the details of a project from the start-up, preparing the way for a smooth and positive construction experience.

SAFETY:Al Ajllan is sincerely interested in the safety of their employees, the public, and our operations. They have a Safety Program and their employees are continually trained on safety issues.
